Identification of Transportation Battery Systems for Recycling Recommended Practice

Year:  DOI ↗

Main topic: battery system

Abstract
The chemistry identification system is intended to support the proper and efficient recycling of rechargeable battery systems used in transportation applications with a maximum voltage greater than 12V (including SLI batteries). Other battery systems such as non-rechargeable batteries, batteries contained in electronics, and telecom/utility batteries are not considered in the development of this specification.
